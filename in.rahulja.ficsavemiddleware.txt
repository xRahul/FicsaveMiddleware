Auto Name:Ficsave Middleware
Summary:Share url of the fanfic and automatically download or email it as ebook
Description:
This is a wrapper around the ficsave.xyz website.  Opening the website, entering
the url, choosing options can become tedious after you've read your 100th
fanfiction. This helps you automate the entire process.

This app supports Android Marshmallow (6.0+) and is compiled against Android Oreo.


## Current Features
* Share fic url with the app to automate what happens.
* choose a default eBook format for further actions.
* Download ebook to device or send as attachment directly from ficsave website.
* Open downloaded eBook by default, or send as attachment via your email app to someone.
* Added deep link to ficsave.xyz
* Download History can be viewed now
